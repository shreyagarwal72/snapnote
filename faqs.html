FAQs

  • Is SnapNote really free and private?
    Yes! SnapNote is always free. All notes are stored only in your browser using local storage—they never leave your device.
  • How does autosave work?
    Your note is saved automatically every few seconds in your device’s browser storage. Refresh or close the page, and your latest draft stays safe.
  • Can I format text and add images?
    Yes! Use the toolbar for bold, italic, underline, lists, colors, links, and inline images. Note: image uploads may be browser-dependent and will not be saved permanently.
  • Is SnapNote mobile friendly?
    Absolutely. SnapNote is designed for smooth use on any phone, tablet, or desktop browser.
  • How do I download or restore my notes?
    Tap “Save” to download your note as a .txt file, “Restore” to bring back your last autosaved note, or “Clear” to remove all content.
  • Does SnapNote work offline?
    SnapNote works as long as the page loads once. Your notes are stored offline until you clear your browser’s data.
  • Is my data safe?
    Your data never leaves your device and is not uploaded to any server. For sensitive notes, back up regularly and stay aware of browser/device privacy.
